Community

Most of the development happens on github: Nim repo and issues

Forum

Nim's forum is were you can go to discuss all things nim.

The forum is fully written in nim, source can be found on github: nimforum

IRC

Come find us on freenode, in #nim. We also have logs, going back to May 2012 !

There's also a channel on gitter, bridged to IRC.

StackOverflow

When asking a question related to Nim, be sure to use the Nim tag in your question.
